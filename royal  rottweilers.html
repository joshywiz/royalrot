Our Story

Royal Rottweilers was founded in 2010 by John and Sarah Thompson, passionate Rottweiler enthusiasts with over 20 years of combined experience in breeding and training these magnificent dogs.

What began as a hobby quickly grew into a mission to preserve and enhance the Rottweiler breed. We focus on breeding dogs with stable temperaments, excellent health, and the classic Rottweiler appearance that adheres to breed standards.

Our Breeding Philosophy

  • Health testing for all breeding dogs (hips, elbows, heart, eyes)
  • Limited breeding to ensure quality over quantity
  • Early neurological stimulation and socialization
  • Lifetime support for all our puppy owners

"Our goal isn't just to sell puppies, but to carefully match each dog with the perfect forever home where they'll thrive as beloved family members."

About the Rottweiler Breed

Understanding the characteristics and needs of this noble breed.

Breed Characteristics

  • Temperament: Confident, courageous, calm, and loyal
  • Size: Males 24-27" tall, 95-135 lbs; Females 22-25" tall, 80-100 lbs
  • Energy Level: Moderate to high, requires daily exercise
  • Trainability: Highly intelligent and eager to please
  • Good With: Proper socialization needed with children and other pets

Care Requirements

  • Diet: High-quality large breed formula, 3-4 cups daily (adult)
  • Grooming: Weekly brushing, seasonal shedding
  • Exercise: Minimum 1-2 hours daily, mental stimulation crucial
  • Training: Early socialization and obedience training essential
  • Health: Prone to hip dysplasia, regular vet checks recommended

Considering a Rottweiler?

Rottweilers are magnificent dogs but require committed owners who understand their needs. They thrive with consistent training, socialization, and plenty of exercise. If you're ready for a loyal, intelligent companion who will protect your family with their life, a Rottweiler might be perfect for you.
